The Galilean Moons

The four bright Galilean moons of Jupiter, Io, Europa, Ganymede and Callisto, and their shadows, are shown below.

Click one by one on the specified moon to record its x-location in pixels into the table below. You will automatically cycle to the next observation date. If you make a mistake, click on the date you want to change and fill in the moons again.

Once you have filled in the table, click Make Plots below.

See just under the diagram for the size of Jupiter on the image in pixels. Later, you can use that to convert your radii in pixels to kilometres.
