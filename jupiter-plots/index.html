Galilean Moon Plots

In the table below, enter the orbital radius (a, in AU) and the orbital period (P, in days) for each moon, based on your measurements in the previous task.

Once you have filled in the table, click 'Make Plot' below it.
You will get a plot of your values for the moons (red points), together with a best fit straight line through them, and another set of points and a line (in green) for the Solar System planets (you don't need to type in any values for the planets).
Then click on the 'Make a PDF from this page' button to save your table and plots for handing in.
